Math Contest Club President

Math Contest Club is the body that organizes CEMC / AMC contests for students at Port Moody Secondary. I have been avidly taking the contests every year myself and improved my problem-solving skills through the process. We also hold meetings to discuss and seek help for challenging questions.

Noons Creek Hatchery Volunteer

With love and care for the environment, the Noons Creek Salmon Hatchery raises coho and chum salmons from eggs to fingerlings before releasing them. I was a lab specialist who tests water samples weekly to ensure that the local stream waters are suitable for the salmons' growth. Every year, I participated in the annual egg-takes to source the eggs from returning salmons. I also helped out in the Fingerling Festivals that invite the public to release the baby salmons and raise awareness for environmental protection.

Port Moody Badminton Co-Captain & Tournament Organizer

Since 2015, I have been heavily involved in the badminton teams at Port Moody Secondary. I helped run the annual invitational tournament in 2017 and 2018 that hosted 8 schools, and was the tournament organizer for the 2019 tournament. I also had the honor to be the coach for the junior team and the co-captain for the senior team in 2019. With my co-captain and sponsor teachers, we held practices, decided team strategies, and unified team spirit. Our team was proud to eventually take third place in the 2019 Provincial Championship!
